Kadayawan Festival: During August and September

The Kadayawan festival is the biggest and most popular festival in Mindanao. This is a thanksgiving festival for the abundance of FRUITS and FLOWERS during the months of August September and all the way to November...A lot of activities are normally lined up during this festivities and this include Street Dancings, Floral Floats Parade, River Floats, and a nightly performance of Artists and Bands at the Rizal Park in front of City Hall.
